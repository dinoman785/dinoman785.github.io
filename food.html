one recipe i know, is:

John Doe's excellent bean dish

part 2

(for two servings)

ingredients:

one can beans, a quarter cup cheese, half a table spoon oregano, half a table spoon salt, half a table spoon garlic or garlic powder, half a table spoon onion or onion powder, and a rotisserie chicken.

step one:

mince garlic, and chop onion. open bean cans, strain, than place in a pot, fill pot up to just over the beans with water, place pot on stove over medium heat, add oregano salt garlic and onion, wait for about five minutes,(or until water has receded)

step two:

remove part of the chicken breast, shred chicken and place in pot with beans. stir until coated, and add cheese. stir until cheese is melted, place in bowl and enjoy! optionaly serve over rice, with salsa and avocado.
